Meet the Team

Problem Statement?

Community building for everyone who has/have been facing problems due to the COVID situation in our country.

What is this project going to offer?

  • Covid-19 has had various impacts on our lives in different ways.
  • Our country has been the first one to report 80,000 cases in a day and Corona Virus has entered into several homes unexpectedly.
  • With this we decided to come up with a project that provides a one stop destination to real time statistics , helping you manage isolation in your homes, providing you the best resources and volunteers during this time and a place where you could vent out through your blogs.

Key Features of Our Website

  • Real time COVID-19 Statistics.
  • Our Heroes : Honouring and Appreciating COVID-19 Warriors and heroes whoâ€™ve helped the community to stay strong and fight the situation( one can register as a hero too)

It can also be considered as a place where you can vent out anonymously.
  • Volunteer : This service helps you to register as a volunteer to help someone and people visiting the website can contact you in case they need any help.
  • Problem Faced and Solutions : Any need or requirements can be posted here and any person or organization willing to help can contact the concerned person to provide their services.
